SitePrice Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: 13 April 2026

1. Introduction

SitePrice ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the SitePrice mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

This privacy policy applies to all users of the SitePrice App. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Photos

The App allows you to capture photographs of job sites to assist with estimate generation. These photos are:

  • Stored locally on your device by default
  • Sent to our AI processing service only when you request an estimate
  • Used solely for the purpose of generating cost estimates

2.2 Microphone / Voice Descriptions

You may provide voice descriptions of jobs or work requirements. Voice data is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Sent to our AI processing service only when needed to generate estimates
  • Converted to text for analysis and estimate generation

2.3 Device Information

We automatically collect limited device information:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • Unique device identifier
  • Mobile network information (if available)

2.4 Usage Analytics

We collect anonymised usage data to improve the App:

  • Features accessed and frequency
  • Estimate generation patterns
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Session duration and app interactions

2.5 Account Information

If you subscribe to a paid tier, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Subscription tier and billing information
  • Payment transaction records

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Estimate Generation: To process your photos and voice descriptions through our AI service to create cost estimates
  • Service Improvement: To understand how you use the App and improve functionality
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and resolve issues with the App
  • Billing and Subscription Management: To process payments and manage your subscription
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity or misuse
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and AI Processing

4.1 AI Service Providers

To generate estimates, your photos and voice descriptions are transmitted to third-party AI services. These services:

  • Are bound by strict data protection agreements
  • Use your data solely for processing your estimate requests
  • Do not train models on your personal data without explicit consent
  • Comply with UK GDPR requirements

4.2 No Data Sales

We explicitly do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your photos or voice recordings with marketers or advertisers
  • Use your data for purposes other than those stated in this policy
  • Share your contact information without your explicit consent

4.3 Service Providers and Partners

We may share limited information with:

  • Payment processors (for billing purposes only)
  • Cloud storage providers (for backup and analytics, where applicable)
  • Analytics services (anonymised data only)

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device in a secure SQLite database:

  • Photos, voice descriptions, and estimate history are retained on your phone
  • You have full control over your local data
  • Data is protected by your device's native security features (encryption, biometrics, etc.)

5.2 Cloud Storage

Currently, SitePrice does not automatically sync your data to cloud servers. All data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to back up or export.

5.3 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure API communication with AI services
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

6.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data. Contact us to submit a data subject access request.

6.2 Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your data. The simplest way to delete all SitePrice data is to uninstall the App, which removes all locally stored information.

6.3 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format. We can export your estimate history and metadata upon request.

6.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data.

6.5 Right to Object

You may object to certain data processing activities.

6.6 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address provided in Section 7.
